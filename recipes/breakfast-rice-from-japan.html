Breakfast Rice from Japan

Description

This breakfast rice recipe is quick to make and delicious. You can use chilled white or brown rice. Adjust the amount of ingredients for your hunger needs!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup cold cooked rice, or more to taste
  • 1 egg, or more to taste
  • 2 green onions, sliced, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Spread rice in a flat layer in hot oil; cook until crispy on the bottom, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Stir egg into rice until scrambled, about 2 minutes more. Sprinkle green onions on top and season with soy sauce.
